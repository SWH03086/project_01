Senior Software Developer

Position Overview

We are seeking an experienced Senior Software Developer to join our dynamic engineering team. This role offers the opportunity to work on cutting-edge projects and mentor junior developers.

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and develop scalable software solutions
  • Lead code reviews and ensure best practices
  • Mentor junior team members
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams
  • Contribute to technical architecture decisions

Required Qualifications

  1. Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field
  2. 5+ years of professional software development experience
  3. Proficiency in JavaScript, Python, or Java
  4. Strong understanding of software design patterns
  5. Excellent problem-solving and communication skills

Benefits

  • Competitive salary and bonus structure
  • Comprehensive health insurance
  • Flexible work arrangements
  • Professional development budget

UX/UI Designer

Position Overview

Join our creative team as a UX/UI Designer and help shape user experiences that delight our customers. You'll work closely with product managers and developers to create intuitive, beautiful interfaces.

Key Responsibilities

  • Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity mockups
  • Conduct user research and usability testing
  • Develop and maintain design systems
  • Collaborate with developers to ensure design implementation
  • Present design concepts to stakeholders

Required Qualifications

  1. 3+ years of experience in UX/UI design
  2. Strong portfolio demonstrating design process and outcomes
  3. Proficiency in Figma, Sketch, or Adobe XD
  4. Understanding of HTML/CSS basics
  5. Experience with user research methodologies

Preferred Skills

  • Motion design and animation experience
  • Knowledge of accessibility standards (WCAG)
  • Experience with design tokens and component libraries
